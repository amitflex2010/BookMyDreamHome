BookMyDreamHome(BMDH)

Moving to a new country or city can be a challenging experience. That's why we're here - to give you access to the widest range of rental properties and services in the Netherlands.

BookMyDreamHome(BMDH) is the leading website for buy/sell apartments, working alongside selected international and national partners to offer peace of mind, quality, and a great service experience.

This application is build with Ionic 2, Angular 2, and Node.js
